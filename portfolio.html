InSafe

InSafe is a people counter aimed at the business management system or any closed area that requires to know in real time the number of people who are inside its establishment. InSafe allows, through a mobile application, to show the data obtained from the sensors previously installed, in addition to graphically showing what that flow has been throughout the day, and it also allows to see the history of all the movements or activations of the sensors.
